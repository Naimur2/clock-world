Embedded localized timetables

Help your visitors by embedding a localized timetable on your own website. These pages detect the timezone your visitor is in and show all times in your visitors timezone. Follow the example below to embed a localized timetable on your website.

How to embed a timetable into your website

  • Step 1: Choose a timetable that you want to embed from the list below
  • Step 2: Copy the embed snippet into your website html, somewhere between the and tags.
  • Step 3: Save your file and refresh your page, you should see the timetable now. You can adjust the height and with by adjusting the values in the snippet you copied.
